Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

关于数据删除 / About Data Deletion

Prime Anchor Workforce（"公司"）尊重您对个人数据的控制权。根据《加州消费者隐私法》（CCPA/CPRA）、《伊利诺伊生物特征信息隐私法》（BIPA）及其他适用法律，您有权要求删除我们持有的您的个人信息。

Prime Anchor Workforce respects your right to control your personal data. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA), and other applicable laws, you have the right to request deletion of your personal information that we hold.

如何提交删除申请 / How to Submit a Deletion Request

1

发送删除申请 / Submit Your Request

通过电子邮件发送删除申请至 info@primeanchorpoint.com，邮件主题注明：数据删除申请 / Data Deletion Request

Send your deletion request via email to info@primeanchorpoint.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request

2

身份验证 / Identity Verification

为保护您的数据安全，我们需要验证您的身份。请在邮件中提供：全名、注册邮箱/电话、账号用户名（如有）。

To protect your data, we need to verify your identity. Please include: full name, registered email/phone, and account username (if applicable).

3

处理时间 / Processing Time

我们将在收到申请后 45个工作日 内处理您的请求，并发送确认邮件。如需延期，我们会提前告知。

We will process your request within 45 business days of receipt and send a confirmation email. If an extension is needed, we will notify you in advance.

4

删除确认 / Deletion Confirmation

完成删除后，我们将发送书面确认，说明哪些数据已被删除，哪些数据因法律要求需要保留。

Upon completion, we will send a written confirmation specifying what data was deleted and what data must be retained due to legal requirements.

可删除的数据 / Data That Can Be Deleted

应您的申请，我们可以删除以下类型的数据（在法律允许的范围内）：

  • 账户信息（用户名、密码、联系方式）/ Account information
  • 申请记录和简历 / Job applications and resumes
  • 身份验证照片（自拍）/ Identity verification photos (selfies)
  • 平台使用数据和日志 / Platform usage data and logs
  • 消息记录和沟通记录 / Message history and communications

必须保留的数据 / Data We Must Retain

部分数据因法律义务无法立即删除，包括：

账户注销 / Account Deletion

若您希望注销 Prime Anchor Workforce 平台账户，请通过以下方式申请：

  • 登录后在"我的信息"页面提交账户注销申请
  • 发送邮件至 info@primeanchorpoint.com

To delete your Prime Anchor Workforce platform account, submit a request through your profile page or email us. Note that account deletion does not automatically delete all personal data — a separate data deletion request may be needed for complete removal subject to retention requirements above.

适用的州级和联邦法律 / Applicable Laws

This policy is designed to comply with:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA)
  • Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA) — 740 ILCS 14/
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) — 15 U.S.C. § 1681
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles (for applicable users)
  • Illinois Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)
