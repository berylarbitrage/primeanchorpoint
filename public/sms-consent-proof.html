During registration, the worker provides a phone number and may check the SMS consent checkbox. The checkbox is unchecked by default and is optional — it is not a condition of employment or account creation. The exact consent language displayed on the live form is:

Step-by-Step Opt-In Flow

1
User visits /register and selects "Worker" registration type.
2
User fills in name, email, phone number, and other required fields.
3
User optionally checks the SMS consent checkbox (unchecked by default). The full disclosure text — including program name, message types, frequency, rates, STOP/HELP instructions, and links to SMS Terms and Privacy Policy — is displayed directly next to the checkbox. Checking is optional and not required to register.
4
User submits the registration form. The consent status (sms_consent: true/false) and a timestamp are recorded in our database.
5
Recurring transactional SMS (job assignment alerts, shift reminders, onboarding updates, interview notifications, payroll alerts) are sent only to users who checked the consent checkbox.

3. Verification Codes (OTP) vs. Recurring SMS

Prime Anchor Workforce sends two categories of text messages. It is important to distinguish between them:

  • Recurring transactional SMS — Job assignment alerts, shift reminders, onboarding updates, interview notifications, payroll alerts, and responses to user inquiries. These are sent only after the user explicitly opts in by checking the SMS consent checkbox on the registration page.
  • One-time verification codes (OTP) — Account registration verification, login verification, and identity verification codes. These are sent only when the user initiates an account action (such as registering, logging in, or verifying identity). OTPs do not require the SMS consent checkbox and are standard security messages triggered by user action.

Opting out of recurring SMS via STOP does not prevent one-time verification codes that the user initiates.

4. Message Types and Frequency

Transactional / Operational Messages Only

  • Verification Codes: Account registration, login, and identity verification
  • Job Assignment Alerts: New assignment details including location, time, and supervisor
  • Shift Reminders: Upcoming shift start reminders and schedule changes
  • Onboarding Updates: Document submission reminders, background check status, and onboarding step completions
  • Interview Notifications: Interview scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Payroll Alerts: Pay period reminders and direct deposit notifications
  • Direct Responses to User Inquiries: Replies to messages or inquiries submitted by the user

We do not send marketing or promotional messages. All SMS is transactional and directly related to the user's employment, application, or direct inquiry.

Message frequency varies based on user activity, assignments, onboarding status, and direct inquiries.
