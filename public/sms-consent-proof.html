1. Worker Registration — Primary Opt-In (Explicit Checkbox)

Live Registration Page

Workers register at the following publicly accessible URL:

https://www.primeanchorpoint.com/register

During registration, the worker provides a phone number and may check the SMS consent checkbox. The checkbox is unchecked by default and is optional — it is not a condition of employment or account creation. The exact consent language displayed is:

This consent language is displayed in all three supported languages (English, Chinese, Spanish) based on the user's language selection.

Opt-In Flow (Step by Step)

1
User visits /register and selects "Worker" registration type
2
User fills in name, email, phone number, and other required fields
3
User optionally checks the SMS consent checkbox (unchecked by default). The full disclosure text — including message types, STOP/HELP instructions, frequency, rates, and links to SMS Terms & Privacy Policy — is displayed directly next to the checkbox. Checking is optional and not required to register.
4
User submits the registration form. The consent status (sms_consent: true/false) and a timestamp are recorded in our database.
5
Recurring operational SMS (job alerts, shift reminders, onboarding updates, payroll alerts) are sent only to users who checked the consent box. One-time verification codes (OTP) may be sent separately when the user initiates an account action such as login or identity verification.

4. Message Types and Frequency

We send only transactional / operational messages

  • Verification Codes: Account registration, login, identity verification
  • Job Assignment Alerts: New assignment details including location, time, and supervisor
  • Shift Reminders: Upcoming shift start reminders and schedule changes
  • Onboarding Updates: Document submission reminders, background check status, onboarding step completions
  • Interview Notifications: Interview scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Payroll Alerts: Pay period reminders and direct deposit notifications
  • Direct Responses to User Inquiries: Replies to messages or inquiries submitted through website forms

We do not send marketing or promotional messages. All SMS is transactional and directly related to the user's employment, application, or direct inquiry.

Message frequency varies based on user activity, assignments, onboarding status, and direct inquiries.

5. Opt-Out and Help

  • Users can reply STOP at any time to opt out of all messages
  • Users can reply HELP to receive program information and support contact
  • Opt-out and help keywords are processed automatically
  • All messages include opt-out instructions
