About Prime Anchorpoint

Prime Anchorpoint is an industrial staffing company built for speed and accountability. We serve warehousing, logistics, manufacturing, and light industrial clients with a trilingual management team (English, Chinese, Spanish) that eliminates communication barriers.

Every worker is background-checked, safety-trained, and ready to perform. Every client gets a dedicated point of contact. No runaround, no excuses.

5 workers or 500. Temp or permanent. We deliver.
