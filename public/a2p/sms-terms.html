1. Who Sends These Messages

Text messages are sent by Prime Anchor Point, an industrial staffing company based in Illinois. Messages are sent from a dedicated 10-digit long code (10DLC) registered phone number.

2. Types of Messages We Send

Recurring transactional SMS (sent only to users who opt in by checking the consent checkbox):

  • Job Assignment Alerts: Notifications when you are assigned to a job, including location, start time, and supervisor contact
  • Shift Reminders: Shift start reminders, schedule changes, and cancellation notices
  • Onboarding Updates: Document submission reminders, background check status, and onboarding step completions
  • Interview Notifications: Interview scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Payroll Alerts: Pay period reminders and direct deposit notifications
  • Direct Responses to User Inquiries: Replies to messages or inquiries submitted through website forms

One-time verification codes (OTP) — these do not require the SMS consent checkbox and are independent of recurring SMS consent:

  • Account Registration Verification: One-time passcode sent when you register a new account
  • Login Verification: One-time passcode sent when you sign in to your account
  • Identity Verification: One-time passcode sent during identity confirmation steps

OTPs are standard security messages triggered solely by your own account actions. They are sent regardless of SMS consent checkbox status. Opting out of recurring SMS via STOP does not prevent OTPs that you initiate.

We do not send unsolicited promotional or marketing messages. All messages are transactional or operational in nature and related to your employment or job application with Prime Anchor Point.

3. How to Opt In

You opt in to receive text messages by visiting our opt-in page at https://www.primeanchorpoint.com/a2p/register and manually checking the optional SMS consent checkbox. The checkbox is unchecked by default and is not required to create an account or seek employment.

By checking the SMS consent checkbox and providing your mobile phone number, you authorize Prime Anchor Point to send you text messages at the number provided. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of employment or account creation.

Opt-In Consent Language (as displayed on the opt-in form)

By checking this box, you agree to receive transactional SMS from Prime Anchor Point Workforce Notifications, including job assignment alerts, shift reminders, onboarding updates, interview notifications, payroll alerts, and responses to your direct inquiries. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. View SMS Terms and Privacy Policy. Consent is not a condition of employment or account creation.

4. How to Opt Out

You can opt out of text messages at any time using any of the following methods:

STOP

Reply STOP to any message to opt out immediately from all recurring messages

CANCEL

Reply CANCEL to opt out of all recurring SMS communications

UNSUBSCRIBE

Reply UNSUBSCRIBE to opt out of all recurring messages

QUIT

Reply QUIT to stop all future messages

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. After that, no further recurring messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

Note: Opting out of recurring SMS does not prevent one-time verification codes (OTP) sent when you initiate an account action such as registering, logging in, or verifying your identity. OTPs are user-initiated security messages that do not depend on the recurring SMS consent checkbox. See Section 2 for the full distinction.
